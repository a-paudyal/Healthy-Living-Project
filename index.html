Insight to Obesity


What is obesity?

Obesity is a complex disease involving an excessive amount of body fat.

It is not just a matter of looks rather it can cause other diseases such as, high blood

pressure, high or low cholesterol, and type 2 diabetes.


What is Our Goal

Our goal with this website is to inform people on the topic of obesity.

We try to help people learn about obesity and we help you get started on

knowing what to do and help start your research on the topic.


Help with Obesity
